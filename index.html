About Jason Ricketts

Jason is a portrait artist and art educator based in May Pen, Jamaica. As the founder of Jae Art, he creates hand-drawn and painted portraits that capture personality, emotion, and story. He specializes in graphite, colored pencil, and acrylic artworks for individuals, couples and families

With a background in teaching and a deep love for the human face, Jason brings patience, precision, and heart to every piece. His work focuses on lifelike expression, subtle emotion, and the beauty found in everyday people.

He is currently writing an art book for high school students, helping young artists develop meaningful and accessible portrait drawing skills.

Jae Art offers a range of portrait options, from mini sketches to full commissions praised for their warmth, accuracy, and emotional depth. Perfect for gifts, memorials, or just honoring someone special.

When he’s not drawing, Jason teaches art to middle schoolers, shares his process on TikTok, and explores how art connects us all.

Artist Statement

As a portrait artist, my work is rooted in story telling. I draw not just faces, but moments capturing the quiet power of expression, the subtle grace in everyday people, and the beauty in simply being seen. My medium of choice ranges from pencil and colored pencil to paint, allowing me to explore texture, light, and intimacy through each stroke. Jason was born from a desire to make portraiture personal again to help others see themselves, or their loved ones, reflected with warmth and honesty. Every piece I create is a collaboration between observation and empathy, crafted with care and attention to the details that make each subject unique. Alongside my practice, I teach art to young students, believing that drawing faces teaches us more than technique it teaches us to look closer, to slow down, and to understand others. Whether I'm working on a commissioned portrait or writing my educational art book, my goal remains the same: to make art that connects.
